Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how COOLCLIP ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application and our integration with the TikTok Content Posting API.

1. Information We Access

When you use COOLCLIP to share content to TikTok, we access certain information via TikTok's OAuth service:

  • Authentication Data: Your TikTok Open ID and access tokens required to authorize video uploads.
  • Video Content: The specific video files you select from your device to be uploaded to your TikTok account.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your privacy is our priority. COOLCLIP uses your data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate the seamless transfer and publishing of your videos to TikTok.
  • To verify your authorization status for the Content Posting API.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third parties. Your video files are processed in real-time for transmission and are not permanently stored on our servers.

3. Data Retention and Security

We implement robust technical measures to ensure your data is secure during the upload process. Once the video is successfully transmitted to TikTok's servers, COOLCLIP does not retain a copy of your video file or sensitive session data.

4. Third-Party Integration

Our service integrates with TikTok. By using COOLCLIP, you acknowledge that your content will also be subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy and Community Guidelines.

5. User Rights

You can revoke COOLCLIP's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok App's "Security and Login" settings or by contacting us directly.
